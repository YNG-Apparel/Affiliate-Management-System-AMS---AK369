Status: Draft

Last Updated: 13 July 2026

# 1. Project Overview

Affiliate Management System (AMS) is an enterprise web-based platform designed to centralize the management of affiliate operations.

The platform replaces manual processes currently handled using spreadsheets, messaging applications, and disconnected systems by providing one integrated solution for affiliate onboarding, content distribution, sales monitoring, commission calculation, payroll management, and performance reporting.

The system consists of two applications connected through a shared backend:

- Manager Dashboard (Web Application)
- Affiliate Portal (Progressive Web Application)

---

# 2. Vision Statement

Build a scalable, secure, and maintainable Affiliate Management System that automates affiliate business operations while reducing manual work and improving operational visibility.

---

# 3. Business Objectives

The system aims to:

- Centralize affiliate management
- Automate commission calculation
- Automate payroll processing
- Simplify affiliate onboarding
- Improve sales visibility
- Reduce manual administrative work
- Support business growth beyond 500 affiliates

Responsible for managing affiliates, products, payroll, reports, and business configuration.

The following items are not included unless requested in future phases:

# 9. Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if:

- Managers can manage affiliates from one platform.
- Affiliates can access assigned content independently.
- Commission calculations are automated.
- Payroll reports are generated automatically.
- Business reports are available in real time.
- Manual spreadsheet usage is significantly reduced.
